About Us

Our Mission

At AniQuest Animal Shelter, we are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned and neglected animals. Our mission is to provide a safe haven for animals in need and to promote responsible pet ownership within our community.

Who We Are

AniQuest is a website that connects many animal shelters around the U.S. with people who are looking to adopt and rescue animals. Our goal is to streamline the adoption process and help find loving homes for as many animals as possible.

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate animal lovers, our team consists of volunteers, foster families, and animal care professionals who work tirelessly to ensure that every animal receives the love and care they deserve.

What We Do

We provide shelter, medical care, and training for our animals while actively seeking loving homes for them. We also engage with the community through educational programs about animal care and welfare, helping to create a more compassionate world for our furry friends.

Get Involved

Your support can make a significant difference in the lives of our animals. Whether you choose to volunteer, foster a pet, or make a donation, you are helping us fulfill our mission of finding forever homes for animals in need.
